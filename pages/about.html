How our company was founded

  • About a year ago I started drawing on my ipad and uploading my artwork onto tik-tok.
    A family member mentioned that they would love a t-shirt with some of my art.
    That is how it all it all began. From T-shirts we expanded to
    stickers/photos/notebooks/planners.
    We hope to provide quality products to all of our customers and enjoy the community.


  • Here at Selcy's Anime Shop All of our digital artwork is handrawn on my ipad pro.


  • T-shirts graphics are printed out and heat pressed on.


  • Stickers are printed out and cut by our to shape by the cricut machine


  • Notebook covers are handrawn.


  • We hope you enjoy all of our handmade Items.
